  • "Stay Hydrated"

    Keep a water bottle at your desk and drink water regularly throughout the day to stay hydrated and maintain focus.

  • "Rest Your Eyes"

    Follow the 20-20-20 rule - every 20 minutes, take a 20-second break to look at something 20 feet away to reduce eye strain and prevent digital eye fatigue.

  • "Move Regularly"

    Incorporate short stretching or movement breaks into your schedule every hour to improve circulation, reduce muscle tension, and boost energy levels.

  • "Practice Mindfulness"

    Take a few minutes each day to practice deep breathing or mindfulness exercises to reduce stress, enhance concentration, and promote overall well-being.

  • "Maintain Proper Posture"

    Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor and your monitor at eye level to support good posture and prevent discomfort or strain in your neck, back, and shoulders.

  • "Nourish Your Body"

    Fuel your body with nutritious snacks and meals to maintain energy levels and focus throughout the day. Choose healthy options like fruits, vegetables, nuts, and whole grains to support overall health and productivity.
